Logical

Education: BS in Computer Science - University of Arizona

Skills: Responsive design, HTML5, SCSS and CSS3, javaScript, jQuery, angular.js, custom WordPress themes, Java, Unix, Linux.

I build well coded, smartly designed programs. When I build a website or app, I care about what goes on behind the scenes. I want my code to work and I want it to last.

I've been coding since I was in middle school. In tenth grade I was the nerd who built a robot while on vacation in a remote mountain cabin buried in snow. More recently I taught four years of high school math, physics and robotics. I've been working as a freelance web developer since 2009.

Creative

Education: BA in Art - University of Arizona

I love making things with my hands, from pen-and pencil drawings to origami nautilus-shell lamps[1]. I try to bring creative, artistic solutions to design projects. I have found that design is as much a language as JavaScript or Spanish.

I still do much of my web-design layout and wireframing with a pencil and paper, and I even use a wacom tablet and stylus to work in Photoshop and Illustrator because I find the pencil-like stylus to be quick, intuitive and freeing.

I enjoy the challenge of bringing a creative vision to life, whether it is my own, my clients', or a combination of both. I specialize in building clean, attractive mobile-ready sites for small businesses and artists.
